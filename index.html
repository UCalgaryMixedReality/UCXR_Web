Who We Are

We are the first Canadian post-secondary engineering design team dedicated to exploring extended reality technologies.

UCXR, formerly known as ARmed, is an engineering design team at the University of Calgary dedicated to exploring the frontiers of XR technology.

Extended Reality (XR) Applications

Education & Learning

Interactive 3D models bring learning to life. Our team has crafted detailed human anatomy models that transform education into an immersive experience. Looking ahead, we are exploring a diverse range of subjects, ensuring that learners engage deeply across various fields of study.

Gaming & Entertainment

Turn any environment into a playground with XR games that deliver truly immersive and interactive experiences. At the Laval Virtual 2025 Hackathon, our team brought this vision to life by creating a playful, VR experience designed to engage and immerse users in a new world.

Industrial & Manufacturing

Future applications of our AR headset extend into industrial training, real-time equipment maintenance visualization, and streamlined quality control inspections, offering hands-free support to enhance efficiency and safety in a variety of settings from the factory floor to lab environments.
